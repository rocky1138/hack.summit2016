Help me get to the portal and find my friend!

Create a single, rectangular div to reach the portal.

A div is a box on a website, like our grass or platforms.

Click the Create button above to place your platform.

Then use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move our player to the portal.

Moving your mouse will show X and Y coordinates on the screen in pixels. X is from the left of the screen. Y is from the bottom of the screen.
